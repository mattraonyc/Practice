Visit Nanchang

Wonder around unparalleled scenic areas, magnificient natural landscapes and more than 2,200 years of history in the heart of southeastern China.

Top three activities to do in Nanchang

Take a stroll in Tengwang pavilion garden

The historic Tengwang is the quintessential chillout spot for the locals.

Explore the Nanchang Wave by Nordic Office of Architecture

Modern European spin on a landscape based community centre with a gorgeous 360 degrees city view at top.

Watch a water show during a night out at Bayi Square

The staple local hangout spot Bayi Square plays water show every night, let the water and lights take you away.
